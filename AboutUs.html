VSC Games, a modern and reputed company that is responsible for the core development of 500+ games from 1996 to today


We are a relatively small company working with multiple developing partners around the world and our main goal is to create and entertain by making remarkable and enjoyable games and applications from scratch. We are responsible for the development of over 500 games and applications that are widely available across the world.


Meet our CEO, Robert Vincent.

Robert Vincent has been the CEO of VSC Games since 2007, when the company was acquired by Nintendo Co., Ltd. His open mind and thoughtful reasoning have got us to this point, and made all of our games and applications come to life.


Our Headquarters

Our headquarters are located in the outer urban area of London, near the limit of the Enfield borough. Although very minimalistic and simple looking, our headquarters are very much the heart of our creations and our projects. Built in 1992, this mid-sized building was originally made for a large and freshly new healthcare company which wanted to expand in the United Kingdom.




Our Story

Starting 1995, the previous CEO, known as Jacob Browns, had a somewhat intriguing but conspicuous plan ; he wanted to challenge the already renowned and well-established game console and award-winning manufacturing company Nintendo, by making one of his own and establishing the company across the entire World. As much as the plan sounded amibitious and had very solid base, things felt almost impossible to execute and people were being skeptical.


Following multiple critics and negative opinions regarding the potential of this future company, Browns remained focused and confident, and chose to keep walking on this path, which made us who we are today. During the 1996-1999 period, our company was solely based on video games production for known game studios such as SEGA and Capcom, which turned out to be slightly beneficial to the company's total annual revenue, although it seemed a little off to CEO Browns. Following a very sluggish and stagnant period of activity, the company took a hard turn and made its way to application and software development...


The recruitment of over 50 new employees has changed the company drastically, and that forever. With the new cellular-phone oriented development, we began working with several companies to develop interfaces for different cellular phone models starting 2000 allowing companies to enhance their sales revenue. We were responsible for the development of the old snake game on all the old cellular phones you know! The company's growth from that point began to increase strikingly ; with a 241% increase in total annual revenue, implementation of a social media service and the expansion of our company to Germany and the United States, everything felt impossible for Browns, who was even more ambitious than before and had further plans in his mind...


VSC Games Today

Following the continuous increase in revenue and the exponential growth of the company, Browns' departure in 2006 due to family incident and multiple personal reasons, the firm's fate was unclear and faded ; everything needed a change, and fast. The atmosphere in the headquarters, or even around our multiple business installations around the world, became completely different and contrasted. With the arrival of Vincent as CEO, things started to change over time, such as the motto, the logo, everything that relates to the organization's core started to change, and for good. CEO Vincent has spent a lot of time, pulled all-nighters and worked hard with our business partners to renew the image of the brand. And it worked!


Although the company's production field has changed, it made us who we are today, and helped all of us, employees and associates, rebuild the true personality and image of VSC Games. The firm came back to its roots and began creating various games and applications for several companies around the world. We are responsible for the develoment of the Geography Quiz game that you all know!
